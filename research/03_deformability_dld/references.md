# Project 3 — Literature anchors

## DLD foundations

- L. R. Huang, E. C. Cox, R. H. Austin, J. C. Sturm, "Continuous
  particle separation through deterministic lateral displacement,"
  *Science* **304**, 987 (2004) — invention.
- D. W. Inglis, J. A. Davis, R. H. Austin, J. C. Sturm, "Critical
  particle size for fractionation by deterministic lateral
  displacement," *Lab Chip* **6**, 655 (2006) — first critical-size
  analysis.
- T. Krüger *et al.*, "Deformability-based red-blood-cell sorting
  using deterministic lateral displacement," *Lab Chip* **14**, 2032
  (2014) — closest existing simulation; 3D.
- M. Holm *et al.*, "Separation of parasites from human blood using
  deterministic lateral displacement," *Lab Chip* **11**, 1326 (2011)
  — clinically relevant rigid-sphere baseline.

## Soft-particle DLD and microfluidic sorting

- G. D'Avino & P. L. Maffettone, "Particle dynamics in viscoelastic
  liquids," *J Fluid Mech* **782**, 213 (2015) — review.
- L. Henon, T. Pham, M. Foessel-Vissac, J. Charrier, A. Bui-Xuan,
  "Numerical simulation of deformable particle sorting in DLD
  pillar arrays," *Biomicrofluidics* **11**, 064108 (2017).
- K. Vahidkhah & P. Bagchi, "Microparticle shape effects on
  margination, near-wall dynamics and adhesion in a 3D simulation
  of red blood cell suspension," *Soft Matter* **11**, 2097 (2015).
- M. M. Lashkaripour *et al.*, "Machine learning for design and
  optimization of DLD devices," *Lab Chip* **23**, 2070 (2023).

## Boundary conditions (curved obstacles)

- M. Bouzidi, M. Firdaouss, P. Lallemand, "Momentum transfer of a
  Boltzmann-lattice fluid with boundaries," *Phys. Fluids* **13**,
  3452 (2001) — interpolated bounce-back.

## Membrane mechanics + capsule dynamics

- R. Skalak *et al.*, "Strain energy function of red blood cell
  membranes," *Biophys. J.* **13**, 245 (1973).
- D. Barthès-Biesel, "Motion and deformation of elastic capsules
  and vesicles in flow," *Annu. Rev. Fluid Mech.* **48**, 25 (2016).
- C. Pozrikidis, *Modeling and Simulation of Capsules and Biological
  Cells* (Chapman & Hall, 2003).

## Lane-order / segregation diagnostics

- M. E. Cates *et al.*, "Glass transition in colloids in the
  presence of an attractive interaction," *Phys. Rev. Lett.* **90**,
  048501 (2003) — for lane-order context.
- M. Vissers, A. van Blaaderen, A. Imhof, "Band formation in mixtures
  of oppositely driven colloids," *Phys. Rev. Lett.* **106**, 228303
  (2011) — laning under flow.

## Inertial focusing (Segré-Silberberg, background regime)

- D. Di Carlo *et al.*, "Inertial microfluidics," *Lab Chip* **9**,
  3038 (2009).
- J. M. Martel & M. Toner, "Inertial focusing in microfluidics,"
  *Annu. Rev. Biomed. Eng.* **16**, 371 (2014).
